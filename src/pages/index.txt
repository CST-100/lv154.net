// the story so far                                           {d}02{/d}

started and led [a team called space scout](https://www.spacescout.info/) from 2019 until
2023. broke some barriers, and the news about the [birth of a
new lunar human spaceflight program](https://www.spacescout.info/2021/10/long-term-future-sls/), and [wrote about some
test flights](https://medium.com/p/df3585d9e39d).

also helped start a group called the [hot nozzle society](https://www.hotnozzlesociety.org/) in
2021. did [some branding work](https://www.behance.net/gallery/167117365/Hot-Nozzle-Society-work), [designed](https://locprecision.com/collections/rockets-3-1-diameter/products/sky-fieri) and [flew](https://x.com/lavie154/status/1719034164220231807) some rockets,
helped [organize some events](https://www.hotnozzlesociety.org/previousevents). on my own time, got level 1 and
level 2 certified with the tripoli rocketry association
(#28294). level 3? watch this space.

joined a scrappy lil rocket company called [abl space systems](https://ablspacesystems.com/)
in 2023. got to [shoot some hotfires](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bJiZKsII3HM), helped [take some cool
photos](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7146545723969351680/), poked at some test stands in the desert, and pursued
a bunch of other cool opportunities. switched to the dark side
leading into 2024 and had a fun time selling rockets and other
cool tactically responsive space things with the strategic
development team. got to tell the world about [a not-so-very-fun
day](https://x.com/ablspacesystems/status/1828182472439611528). joined a bunch of friends on an [involuntary 2-month paid
vacation](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7235130720954826753/) in august 2024.

since feb 2025, a few friends and i have been toying with a
project called [{g}Blacklight{/g}](https://goblacklight.com). there's space lasers, optics,
and all sorts of other cool stuff. we'll see where that goes –
if you want to help keep the world running on time, hit my line
and let's talk!
